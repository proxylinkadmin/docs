## Early access

ProxyLink is currently in early access. During this period, all features are available for free — no credit card, no time limit. Paid plans are not yet enforced.

When billing goes live, subscribers who signed up during early access lock in their current rate forever. If prices increase later, you are unaffected as long as you stay subscribed.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a tunnel on the Free plan?">
    No — but without a tunnel, you can only create HTTP/HTTPS proxy links pointing to publicly reachable servers. TCP links, browser SSH/RDP/VNC, and LAN access all require a tunnel (Developer plan or above).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the introductory MSP price?">
    €69/month is the introductory price for early-access subscribers. This rate is locked in for life — you will never be charged more as long as you remain subscribed. If you cancel and resubscribe after prices increase, the new rate applies.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes — the entire platform is free during the early access period. No trial countdown, no credit card needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?">
    Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
