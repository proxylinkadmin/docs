---
title: Session Recording
description: Record SSH, RDP, and VNC sessions for compliance, auditing, and troubleshooting.
---

ProxyLink can record all SSH, RDP, and VNC sessions. Recordings are stored on the server and playable directly in the browser.

## Enable recording

Edit a proxy link and toggle **Session Recording** on.

## Viewing recordings

Go to **More** → **Recordings**. Each session shows the date, user, device, and duration. Click **Play** to replay the session in the browser.

## Storage and retention

| Plan | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Free | Not available |
| Developer | 14 days |
| MSP | 14 days |

Recordings are automatically deleted after the retention period.

<Note>During the current Early Access period, retention is 3 days for all plans.</Note>

## Recording formats

| Protocol | Format |
|----------|--------|
| SSH | asciicast v2 (`.cast`) |
| RDP | Guacamole binary (`.guac`) |
| VNC | Guacamole binary (`.guac`) |

Both formats are playable directly in the ProxyLink browser player. SSH recordings can also be downloaded and played with any asciicast-compatible tool.

## NIS2 compliance

Session recordings contribute to NIS2 Article 21 requirements for audit trails of privileged access. The audit log records who connected, when, from where, and for how long — and links directly to the session recording.
