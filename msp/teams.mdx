---
title: Teams & Roles
description: Manage multiple engineers with role-based access to client sites.
---

ProxyLink teams allow MSPs to give engineers access to specific client groups and devices without sharing admin credentials.

## Team roles

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Full admin access — manage team members, all clients, all devices |
| **Engineer** | Assigned client groups only |
| **Employee** | Assigned devices only |

## Inviting team members

1. Go to **Teams** → your team → **Invite Member**
2. Enter their email address and select a role
3. They receive an invitation email — no ProxyLink account required, they create one during acceptance

## Assigning access

- **Engineers** are assigned to client groups. They can see all devices within their assigned groups.
- **Employees** are assigned to specific devices. They can only access those devices.

Neither role can see clients or devices outside their assignment.

## Plan limits

Teams are available on the **MSP** plan. Team size: up to 5 members (MSP), unlimited (Custom).
