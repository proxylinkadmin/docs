---
title: Asset Inventory
description: A full list of every device and service across all client sites.
---

The asset inventory lists every tunnel, proxy link, and service across all your client groups in one table.

## Access

Go to **More** → **Asset Inventory**.

## Export

Click **Export CSV** to download the full inventory as a spreadsheet. Useful for client reports, audits, and NIS2 documentation.

## What's included

- Device name, type, and VPN IP
- Client group
- All proxy links per device (URL, port, scheme)
- Online/offline status
- Last seen timestamp
