ProxyLink is built for European MSPs operating under NIS2 (Greece Law 5160/2024 and EU Directive 2022/2555). The audit log and compliance portal are designed to meet Article 21 requirements for privileged access monitoring.

## Audit log

Every SSH, RDP, and VNC session is logged with:

- Who connected (user name and email)
- When (timestamp in local time)
- From where (source IP)
- To which device
- Session duration
- Link to session recording (if enabled)

The audit log is **immutable** — entries cannot be edited or deleted. Export as CSV for client reports or regulatory submissions.

Access: **More** → **Audit Log**

## Compliance portal

Generate a client-facing compliance report without giving the client a ProxyLink account:

1. Go to the client group → **NIS2 Compliance Portal**
2. Generate a token-gated URL
3. Share with the client or auditor

The portal shows the audit log for that client's devices only. No other clients' data is visible.

## NIS2 relevance

Under NIS2, your clients' risk assessments must account for their supply chain — including the remote access tools their MSP uses. ProxyLink's compliance portal directly answers that requirement:

- EU-hosted infrastructure (Hetzner Germany)
- Open-source WireGuard protocol — no proprietary black-box routing
- Full session audit trail with recordings
- No third-party routing — traffic stays on EU infrastructure end to end

<Note>
NIS2 Article 21 requires "appropriate measures" for privileged access. ProxyLink's audit log and session recording are designed to satisfy this requirement. Consult your legal advisor for your specific obligations.
</Note>
