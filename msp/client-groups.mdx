---
title: Client Groups
description: Organise tunnels and devices by client site for MSP workflows.
---

Client groups let you organise your tunnels and proxy links by client — one group per site, office, or client.

## Create a group

Go to **Proxy Links** → use the group selector to create a new group. Assign tunnels and proxy links to the group.

## Engineer assignment

Engineers on your team are assigned to groups. They can only see devices within their assigned groups.

## Access links

Each group can have access links — shareable URLs that give a client's staff access to specific devices without a ProxyLink account.
