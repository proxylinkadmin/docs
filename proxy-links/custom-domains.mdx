---
title: Custom Domains
description: Use your own domain for proxy links instead of the default proxy.proxylink.dev subdomain.
---

Attach a custom domain to a proxy link to serve it at your own URL.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a domain in ProxyLink">
    Go to **Domains** → **+ Add Domain**. Enter your domain (e.g. `remote.yourdomain.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a CNAME record">
    In your DNS provider, add a CNAME record:
    ```
    remote.yourdomain.com → proxy.proxylink.dev
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach to a proxy link">
    Edit the proxy link and select your custom domain from the **Attach to domain** dropdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## SSL certificates

ProxyLink automatically provisions and renews Let's Encrypt certificates for custom domains. No manual certificate management needed.
